CHESHMA, Supplier of Afghani products Company

Cheshma UG is a Germany-based trading company specializing in the import and wholesale of carefully selected products, with a current focus on authentic goods from Afghanistan. Founded in 2025, we aim to connect global markets through culturally rich, high-quality merchandise.Our initial product range includes traditional Afghan items such as handicrafts, textiles, dried fruits, and spices, reflecting the countryâ€™s deep heritage and artisanal expertise. We work closely with local producers and suppliers to ensure authenticity, ethical sourcing, and consistent quality.

While our roots are in Afghan trade, our long-term vision is to grow into a diverse international trading platform, offering a wide variety of goods and building lasting partnerships worldwide.

  • We look forward to building new connections and delivering value through global commerce.
  • Authentic Product Sourcing Direct collaboration with Afghan artisans and producers ensures genuine, culturally rich goods.
  • Ethical and Fair Trade Practices Strong commitment to ethical sourcing and fair compensation for local suppliers.

Cultural and Market Expertise Deep understanding of Afghan heritage and its products, giving Cheshma a unique edge in niche markets.

We are a young company with a strong commitment to transparency, fair trade, and customer satisfaction.
